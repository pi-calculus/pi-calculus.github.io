Our Story

A Home Away
From Home

Tucked away from the city's rush, Serenity Haven was born from a simple dream — to offer weary travellers a place where time slows down. Our boutique guest house blends warm, earthy interiors with genuine Sri Lankan hospitality.

Every corner of Serenity Haven has been curated with care — from the hand-picked organic linens to the garden-view breakfast nook. We believe a stay here isn't just accommodation; it's an experience.
